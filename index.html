About Our REU Program

The REU ASSET program at FIU provides undergraduate students with hands-on research experience in cutting-edge areas of advanced sensor technologies, cybersecurity, IoT systems, and emerging technologies. Our program combines theoretical foundations with practical applications in state-of-the-art laboratories.

Students work closely with renowned faculty mentors on projects that address real-world challenges in sensor security, data privacy, wireless systems, and next-generation sensing technologies funded by NSF and DOD.

  • Hands-on research experience
  • One-on-one faculty mentorship
  • Professional development workshops
  • Networking opportunities
  • Conference presentation opportunities
